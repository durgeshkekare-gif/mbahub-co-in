What CU Online MBA does well

  • Price — ₹70–90K total is among the lowest for a NAAC A+ online MBA in India
  • Tech-focused specialisations — Cloud Computing and Cybersecurity electives not commonly found at this price point
  • North India placement network — CU's 38,000+ on-campus students and Mohali location means strong Punjab, Haryana, Delhi NCR hiring connections even for online graduates
  • Faculty quality — CU has invested heavily in online faculty; average satisfaction in online reviews is high

Where CU Online MBA falls short

  • No WES recognition — if Canada PR is a goal, choose Manipal MUJ or Amrita instead
  • No bundled industry certs — Amrita bundles Oracle, NISM, NSE, Amazon at the same price tier
  • Brand recognition outside north India — CU is a strong brand in Punjab/Haryana but less recognised in South India compared to Manipal or Amrita
  • NAAC A+ vs A++ — Amrita and LPU have NAAC A++; CU has A+

Who should choose CU Online MBA

  • Budget-conscious: you want NAAC A+ but want to save ₹35–65K vs Manipal/Amrita
  • North India based: Punjab, Haryana, Delhi NCR — CU's placement connections are strongest here
  • Tech-focused: want Cloud Computing or Cybersecurity specialisation at an affordable price
  • Canada PR is NOT a priority: if it is, go Manipal or Amrita (both WES recognised)
