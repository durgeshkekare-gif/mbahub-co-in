Why LPU Online MBA is underrated

LPU Online MBA gets less coverage than Manipal or Amrita, but it punches above its marketing budget. best online MBA NAAC A+ India+ is the same accreditation grade as Amrita — India's most accredited online university. WES recognition is confirmed — meaning it's valid for Canada Express Entry points calculation. And at ₹80–1L, it costs ₹35,000–55,000 less than Manipal or Amrita for the same accreditation level. That's the LPU advantage in one line.

LPU Online MBA placements — realistic picture

LPU's 5.8 lakh alumni network is one of India's largest private university networks. Top hiring companies for LPU graduates include Infosys, TCS, Wipro, HCL, Amazon, Flipkart, HDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Deloitte, and Bajaj Auto. Online MBA graduates access LPU's placement portal and alumni network. Reported median salary post-MBA: ₹5–8L (fresh/low experience) to ₹10–18L (3+ years experience). LPU is especially strong for IT sector placements given its Punjab tech ecosystem.
