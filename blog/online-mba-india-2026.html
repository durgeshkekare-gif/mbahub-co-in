How to choose

For WES recognition: Symbiosis SSODL and Manipal MUJ are the only two with WES approval — critical if you plan to work or study abroad.

For NAAC A++ at lowest fee: LPU at ₹1.46L is the cheapest NAAC A++ option. Amrita at ₹1.76L adds research credibility. BVDU at ₹1.78L is strong for Maharashtra-based professionals.

For the most specialisations: Chandigarh University leads with 26 specialisations, followed by Jain (22) and Amity (18).

For institutional brand recognition nationally: Symbiosis SSODL is the strongest national brand across industries in India — the parent Symbiosis International University is NAAC A++. Fee of ₹3.7L is the highest in the group but reflects the brand premium.

Validity for government jobs

All UGC-DEB approved programs are valid for government job applications where the notification accepts online degrees. A few legacy notifications still specify "regular mode only" — always verify the specific notification before applying.
