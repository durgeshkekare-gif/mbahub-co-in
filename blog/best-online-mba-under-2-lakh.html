Which should you choose?

Cheapest NAAC A++ option: LPU at ₹1.46L. The most affordable among NAAC A++ universities. 7 specialisations, strong digital infrastructure, Punjab-based university with national recognition.

Most specialisations under ₹2L: Jain University at ₹1.96L. 22 specialisations including niche areas like healthcare management and retail management. NAAC A+.

For Maharashtra-based professionals: BVDU at ₹1.78L. NAAC A++ and strong local employer recognition in Pune and Mumbai. 14 specialisations, including Project Management — rare among online MBAs.

Only WES-approved under ₹2L: Manipal MUJ at ₹1.8L. If you have international career plans (Canada, US, abroad), MUJ is the only under-₹2L option with WES recognition.

What about even cheaper options?

IGNOU's distance MBA starts under ₹1L and is UGC-DEB approved. It is primarily self-paced with limited live faculty interaction — best for cost-driven degree requirement, not for active career advancement. Annamalai University's distance MBA starts at ₹25K but NAAC B++ only.
