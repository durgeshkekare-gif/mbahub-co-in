Manipal University Online MBA Review 2026 — Fee, Curriculum & Is It Worth It?

Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ) online MBA — overview

Manipal University Jaipur's online MBA is one of the most popular online MBA programs in India in 2026. Key reasons: NAAC A+ accreditation, WES (World Education Services) recognition for Canadian immigration purposes, UGC-DEB approval, and a fee of approximately ₹1.35L over 2 years — making it one of the most affordable credible online MBAs available. The programme is delivered on the Manipal Online platform with live sessions, recorded lectures, industry projects and a dedicated placement support team.

Accreditation and recognition

Manipal University Jaipur holds NAAC A+ — among the highest NAAC grades. The online MBA is UGC-DEB approved, meaning it is a valid degree equivalent to any full-time MBA for the purposes of Indian government and corporate employment. WES recognition is particularly valuable for professionals planning to apply for Canadian immigration through Express Entry — MUJ's online MBA is one of the few Indian online MBAs that WES will evaluate for equivalency.

Specialisations available

Manipal online MBA offers specialisations in: Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Operations & Supply Chain, Business Analytics, Data Science & AI, Healthcare Management, Banking & Finance, and International Business. The Business Analytics and Data Science specialisations are among the most popular, combining the MBA credential with technical skills.

Is Manipal online MBA worth it?

For working professionals wanting a credible, affordable online MBA with good accreditation, Manipal MUJ is among the top choices in India. The WES recognition makes it uniquely valuable for those with immigration plans. The Business Analytics specialisation provides practical ROI for professionals in data-adjacent roles. The main limitation is that the Manipal brand does not carry the same weight as IIM or top private B-schools for premium management roles — but for the price point (₹1.35L) and the combination of NAAC A+, UGC-DEB approval and WES recognition, it offers strong value.
