WES Approved Online MBA India 2026 — Who Needs It and Which Programs

What is WES and who needs it?

WES (World Education Services) evaluates international academic credentials for use in Canada, the USA and internationally. A WES evaluation is required for: Canadian Express Entry immigration (IRCC accepts WES-evaluated degrees), US visa applications requiring academic credential assessment, applications to foreign universities, and employment at multinationals requiring international background checks.

If you are staying in India: WES recognition is irrelevant for your career. NAAC grade and UGC-DEB approval are the relevant criteria.

Which to choose for WES?

If WES is your primary requirement and budget matters: Manipal MUJ at ₹1.8L is the clear choice — comparable WES recognition at less than half the cost of SSODL. If you need WES plus NAAC A++ plus the Symbiosis brand for domestic career advancement simultaneously — SSODL at ₹3.7L justifies the premium.

Direct-enrollment WES options

NMIMS (direct enrollment, no partner) also has international recognitions. Verify directly with the institution for current WES evaluation status.
