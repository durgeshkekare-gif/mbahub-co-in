What makes Amrita stand out — the Grant Thornton factor

Amrita's MBA in FinTech is co-designed with Grant Thornton — one of India's big-4 advisory firms. This isn't just a branding exercise. The curriculum includes actual Grant Thornton case studies, guest sessions from GT practitioners, and a GT-network hiring pathway. For professionals targeting consulting, BFSI, or FinTech, this direct connect is a genuine differentiator no other online MBA in India offers.

Who should choose Amrita Online MBA

  • FinTech / Finance professionals — the Grant Thornton MBA is unmatched in this category
  • Canada PR aspirants — NAAC A++ + WES recognised = strongest credential combination
  • Professionals wanting industry certifications bundled — Oracle, NISM, NSE, Amazon at no extra cost
  • Healthcare sector professionals — Amrita's hospital network (6 hospitals, 6,000+ beds) makes healthcare MBA placement uniquely strong

Who should NOT choose Amrita

  • If you need the widest specialisation range — Manipal MUJ has more options (12 vs 6)
  • If ICAS recognition (for UK/Australia) matters — Manipal has ICAS, Amrita does not
  • If you want an IIM brand — choose IIM Nagpur Blended MBA (higher fee, CAT required)
